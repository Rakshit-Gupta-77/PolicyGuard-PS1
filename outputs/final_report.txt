Patch Management Policy (Revised)


**Introduction: Purpose & Scope**
The purpose of this Patch Management Policy is to establish guidelines and procedures that ensure the timely application, testing, verification, documentation, reporting, auditing, configuration management, integrity checking, rollback capability in case an update causes issues (incident response), adherence to security controls (access control) within organizational systems.

**Roles and Responsibilities: Patch Management Team & All Employees**
The IT team will be responsible for installing updates on a monthly basis, with the assistance of all employees who are required to maintain awareness about software changes affecting their work environment or system configurations they utilize. Staff members must report any anomalies encountered post-update immediately to IT department. 

**Access Control: Authorized Access and User Permissions Management**
Only authorized personnel will have the ability to install updates on organizational systems, ensuring that all changes maintain integrity control within our network perimeter (access control). A comprehensive permission audit shall be conducted bi-annually by an independent security firm.
